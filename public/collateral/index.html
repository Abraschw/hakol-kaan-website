Public SMS disclosure

Text START to +1 (917) 590-8002 to receive recurring promotional, sponsored, transactional, and customer-care SMS/MMS messages from Hakol Kaan. The customer sends the first message by texting START. Messages may include onboarding, store menus, item search results, cart and order confirmations, delivery and ride updates, payment/setup and message-credit notices, support replies, and Hakol Kaan promotional or sponsored offers. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to opt out. Consent is not a condition of purchase. We will not sell, rent, trade, transfer, or share your mobile information, mobile opt-in data, or SMS consent with third parties. Terms: https://www.hakolkaan.com/terms. Privacy: https://www.hakolkaan.com/privacy.
