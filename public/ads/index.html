Before pressing submit, please note that your ad goes through Hakol Kaan review before it can run. Fixed ads are charged when accepted. Bids are charged only if they win. If you cancel after payment, the cancellation fee is 4% of the ad price.

What happens next?

  1. Set up payment.
  2. Submit your picture or text ad.
  3. For a fixed-price ad, your payment method is charged when the ad is accepted and placement is available.
  4. For a bidding ad, no hold is placed. Your payment method is charged only if your bid wins.
  5. If your bid wins, your payment method is charged five minutes before the scheduled send.
  6. Open your dashboard to see all your ads, cancel eligible ads, and increase active bids.
