4. Payment and updates

Your full card number is collected on Stripe's secure checkout page and is not stored by Hakol Kaan. Returning from Stripe does not charge you.

What happens next?

  1. Securely save a card through Stripe.
  2. Submit your picture or text ad for Hakol Kaan review.
  3. For a fixed-price ad, your card is charged only after approval and available placement.
  4. For a bidding ad, approval activates your bid after a temporary card check that is released right away.
  5. If your bid wins, your saved card is charged five minutes before the scheduled send.
